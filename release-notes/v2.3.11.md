## Highlights
- **Triple-space typing translate (beta):** type anywhere, hit the spacebar three times, and your draft is translated to English using whichever AI provider you configured. We keep the caret position, show a floating spinner, and surface backend errors inline.

## Install
1. Download `SummarizerX-v2.3.11.zip` from this release.
2. Unzip the archive (do **not** load the ZIP directly).
3. Visit `chrome://extensions`, enable **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and select the unzipped folder.
